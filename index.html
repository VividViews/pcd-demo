What We Offer

Comprehensive Mobile Diagnostic Services

Our board-certified sonographers bring state-of-the-art ultrasound technology directly to your facility, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

🫀
Cardiac Ultrasound
Comprehensive echocardiography to assess heart structure and function, including wall motion, valve assessment, and hemodynamics.
M-Mode Echo 2D Echo Doppler Stress Echo Echo Screenings
🩸
Vascular Ultrasound
Detailed imaging of peripheral vessels to detect clots, plaque, and circulatory conditions affecting the venous and arterial systems.
IMT Venous Doppler (DVT) Arterial Doppler (PAD) ABI
🧠
Carotid Ultrasound
Non-invasive assessment of the carotid arteries to evaluate stroke risk, detect stenosis, and image plaque or abnormal blood flow patterns.
Stroke Risk Evaluation Carotid Artery Imaging Stenosis Detection
🔬
General Ultrasound
Full abdominal, pelvic, thyroid, and soft-tissue imaging performed by certified sonographers for a wide range of diagnostic needs.
Thyroid Abdominal Aorta Renal Artery Breast Liver / Gallbladder Pelvic Testicular Retroperitoneal
🦵
ABI — Ankle Brachial Index
A simple, non-invasive test that compares blood pressure at the ankle and arm to screen for peripheral artery disease (PAD) and assess circulation risk.
PAD Screening Circulation Assessment Non-Invasive
📊
Holter Monitor
Continuous 24-hour EKG recording to capture cardiac arrhythmias, palpitations, and electrical abnormalities during normal daily activity.
24-Hour EKG Arrhythmia Detection Continuous Recording

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about scheduling and working with PCD.

How quickly can a sonographer arrive at my facility?
▼
After an order is placed, our sonographers typically arrive within 40 to 60 minutes. Our team is strategically positioned throughout the Fort Bend and Greater Houston area to ensure rapid response regardless of time of day.
When will I receive the interpretation and report?
▼
Standard reports are available in the physician portal within 24 hours of study completion. For urgent or STAT cases, board-certified interpretations are delivered within 60 minutes of the study being performed.
What types of facilities does PCD serve?
▼
PCD provides mobile ultrasound services to a wide range of settings, including hospitals, outpatient clinics, physician offices, nursing homes, assisted living facilities, and private residences. If a patient needs a study, we find a way to get there.
Are your sonographers certified?
▼
Yes. All PCD sonographers are registered and board-certified in all three ultrasound modalities — Cardiac (RDCS), Vascular (RVT), and General (RDMS). This ensures you receive expert-level imaging for every study type without needing to call multiple providers.
Who interprets the ultrasound studies?
▼
All studies are read by board-certified cardiologists or radiologists, depending on the study type. Final interpreted reports are transmitted directly to your physician portal — ready for immediate clinical use.
Do you operate on weekends and holidays?
▼
Absolutely. PCD operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year — including all weekends and holidays. Our on-call team is always ready to respond to urgent diagnostic needs.
What if my facility is outside the 14-county coverage area?
▼
Our primary coverage covers 14 counties surrounding Fort Bend, but we service all of Texas. For locations outside our standard coverage zone, call us at 713-715-7056 and we'll arrange service for your facility.
